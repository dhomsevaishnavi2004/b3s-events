Get Your Answers Now

Planning an event? We’ve got you covered! Explore our most common questions to know how B3 Events can make your celebration stress-free, stylish, and unforgettable.

What types of events do B3 Events organize?

We specialize in wedding events, haldi and mehndi functions, birthday parties, receptions, and grand celebrations. Whether it’s a traditional setup or a modern theme, we plan everything as per your choice and style.

Do you provide complete event management or only decoration services?

We provide full event management services including mandap decoration, lighting, sound system & DJ, catering, photography, mehndi artists, and special entries for the bride and groom. Everything is managed professionally from start to finish.

Can you customize themes and decorations as per our choice?

Absolutely! Our creative team designs customized themes, color palettes, and décor concepts that match your preferences. From traditional to modern, we make your event truly unique.

How early should we contact you for booking an event?

We recommend booking at least 1–2 months in advance to ensure availability and smooth planning. However, we also handle urgent bookings depending on the event requirements and schedule.
